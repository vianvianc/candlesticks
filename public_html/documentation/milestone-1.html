This project will be an attempt at a professional retail website homepage. The website will specialize in van conversions. I want to focus on front-end development and to integrate as many bootstrap elements as I have content for, including a form, navbar to future links, content on accordian, and background and carosel images.
The audience will be outdoorsy, middle-class, consumers considering hiring a professional to convert their existing or new van for leisure travel. It would be designed for clients and to showcase my front-end experience to potential employers.
My goal is to put some van-build content online and have a fully functioning homepage and contact form with links to other pages in progress. I want to include tags for search engine optimization and optimize the site across all devices/browsers. I want to get an internship/job, attract freelance clients, and have project to showcase during an interview.
