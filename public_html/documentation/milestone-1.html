This project will be an attempt at a professional candlestick trading information homepage. The webpage/homepage will detail the history of candlesticks, how they are presented to traders today and will detail a select few indicators. I want to focus on front-end development and to integrate as many bootstrap elements as I have content for, including a form, navbar to future links, content on accordian, and background and carosel images.
The audience will range from younger day traders often looking for trends on reddit forums to mid-level traders who want to save time and explore new ways to implement their trading strategy or find additional confirmation signals to their existing trading plan. The page will also serve to showcase my front-end experience to potential employers.
My goal is to put some candlestick content online and have a fully functioning homepage and contact form with links to other pages in progress. This is my personal future project where I hope to integrate a financial API where I can analyze historical financial data to validate the candlesticks status as an accurate price predictor. Then I would like to see which indicators are the most accurate and create a real-time candlestick pattern tool which searches for the pattern the day/hour/minute it occurs. I want to include tags for search engine optimization and optimize the site across all devices/ browsers. I want to get an internship/job, attract freelance clients, and have project to showcase during an interview.
